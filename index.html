REO PURGYIL -
      CROWN OF  HIMACHAL

6,816m(22,362ft)
31.806′ N , 78.833′E
Kinnaur , Himachal Pradesh (Near Indo-Tibet Border)

Rising dramatically along the remote India–Tibet frontier, Reo Purgyil stands as the highest peak in Himachal Pradesh and one of the most formidable climbs in the Western Himalaya. This expedition-grade journey leads through stark valleys, glacial moraines, and vast snowfields untouched by mass tourism. Trekkers and climbers face thin air, unpredictable weather, and demanding terrain, making it a true test of endurance and resilience. In return, the mountain offers absolute solitude, pristine alpine beauty, and sweeping views across endless Himalayan ranges. Reaching its slopes is not just an adventure—it is an encounter with raw wilderness at the edge of the sky.
